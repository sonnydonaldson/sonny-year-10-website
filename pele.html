Where he grew up

Pele grew up in the povity city of Bauru, Brazil, in the state of São Paulo even though he was born in Três Corações but he moved to Bauru when he was young. He would work in a tea shop as a servant to earn some extra money. He was taught how to play football by his dad, he could not afford a real football so they would usually use a sock stuffed with newspaper and tied with string or a grapefruit instead.

What made him want to play

The things that made him want to play is a combination of him just having a natural talent for football. His farther also recognized Pele's innate abilities and instilled in him the importance of dedication and practice. He also lived in relative poverty so he was determined to get out of poverty. He had some early success in youth leagues and with Santos FC.
